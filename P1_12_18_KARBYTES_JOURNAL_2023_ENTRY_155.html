I decided to remove the tweet which I took a screenshot of to display as an image in the previous journal entry. I tried to make the value definitions fit inside the maximum number of free account tweet character limit, but in order to do so, I removed words which, according to me, made the final draft appear easy to possibly construe as suggesting that an information processing agent is limited to only being allowed to choose (“at gunpoint”) from a list of menu options which someone other than the information processing agent intentionally created (or which spontaneously emerged from pure nothingness if not from some relatively “unintelligent” process). In other words, the tweet which I deleted seemed easy for me to construe as my life being about being a captive player of some contrived game in which the players are largely unable to alter the game mechanics and, instead, play the game as though the players have no choice but to play that game and as though the players have no creative influence over their universes other than being able to exercise volition merely as a consumer in a gilded prison of a marketplace, arcade, or amusement park.
